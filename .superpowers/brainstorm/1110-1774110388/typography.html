The 4 changes that matter most

1 — Kill excessive ALL CAPS tracking
Current: NAVIGATION · NET WORTH · DATE — hard to read at small sizes.
Fix: sentence-case labels at 11px with modest tracking (0.04em).
2 — Raise secondary text contrast
Current: #374151 and #4b5563 — near-invisible on dark background (fails WCAG AA).
Fix: secondary text → rgba(255,255,255,0.55), tertiary → rgba(255,255,255,0.40).
3 — Tighter letter-spacing on headings
Large bold text reads better with negative tracking (−0.02em) — feels premium and modern vs default spacing.
